    About Me

    Myself Tushar Pal, a small-town dweller from a charming village in West Bengal, India. While my days are filled with engineering studies, my heart belongs to the world of Art. As an amateur painter, I bring my creativity to life on canvas. But as a designer and developer, I'm passionate about crafting digital experiences and I love blending design and code to bring ideas to life.
    Currently I'm a Freelance Designer and Illustrator. I have almost 4 years of working experience in a Digital Marketing based startup as a Graphic Designer and later as a Creative Director. Some of my works are just a scroll away, explore them and let's create something amazing together!
